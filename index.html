About Audrey Mirindi

Audrey Mirindi is a dynamic Tech entrepreneur, Rapper, Song writer and Actor based in Goma, Democratic Republic of Congo (DRC). As the CEO and Founder of Amtech Technology, Audrey is at the forefront of driving digital innovation across Africa. With a deep expertise in robotics, mechatronics, digital marketing, SEO, and full-stack web and mobile development, Audrey is committed to shaping the future of technology in Africa.

Founding Amtech Technology

In 2021, Audrey founded Amtech Technology with a vision to empower African businesses and individuals through cutting-edge technology. The company specializes in AI systems, web and mobile development, cybersecurity, digital marketing, and hardware solutions, aiming to bridge the digital divide in Central and East Africa .

Music and Comedy

Beyond technology, Audrey is passionate about music and comedy. As a musician, Audrey creates music that resonates with audiences, and as a comedian, Audrey performs comedy that brings joy to people everywhere. This blend of creativity and technical expertise allows Audrey to connect with diverse audiences and inspire new possibilities.

Looking Ahead

Audrey's mission is to accelerate technological innovation across Africa and extend its impact globally, empowering businesses and communities through smart, scalable digital solutions. With a focus on locally relevant and globally scalable solutions, Audrey continues to lead Amtech Technology towards a future where African-built platforms compete on the global stage .
